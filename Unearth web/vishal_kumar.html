Vishal Kumar

14years Old, New Delhi


Hello my name is Vishal. I am fourteen years old. I am living in Delhi with my family in a small room . Today I am going to tell you one of my experiences on climate change. Usually climate during summer are seemed to be hotter and days longer while during winter temperatures is low (chill) and nights are longer. During summer vacations I went to a village. When I was exploring the village weather was nice cloudy there were not much but many tresses as compare to city but after ten minutes weather change to hot. In evening temperatures cool down and suddenly it started raining .After an hour rain stop and it become wet and hot climate. At night I wasn't able to sleep then again it started raining I was confused why was it happening. Then next day we went to greet other village people in afternoon the heatwave was too hot that my skin got red and I was feeling dehydrated. My family also suffer from heat exhaustion . Street animals were also try to find shade to save themselves from heatwaves. We find a pond with little amount of water. Village farmers were also worried about their crops due to unexpected heavy rainfall which can wash away soil nutrients and damage crop . When we reached home I was curious why weather was changing in village so I searched. I found that climate change can affect human and also others coexist. The main reason is an increase in green house gases like chlorofluorocarbon ,Methane, Nitrous oxide, carbon dioxide etc. in atmosphere Deforestation to develop human settlements. Excessive usage of fossil fuels and livestock farming which leads to global warming and climate change. Global warming doesn't mean the earth is getting warmer it massively effecting social,economic and physical health are area for greater distress. Many people are aware about it but still not concern to do anything about it . Last month teacher told us about global warming, it's effect and how our small action can help to reduce global warming like by recycling thing, reduce water waste, by using public transport or bicycle for traveling purposes, by switching off lights before leaving house, throw away less, planting a tree etc. We were asked to plant a tree in school.
