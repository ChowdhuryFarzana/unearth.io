Raj Shah

11th grade
Kishangarh, Vasant Kunj ,New Delhi, India


Greetings! Hi, My name is Raj Shah and I am a student of class 11th. I live in Delhi with my little brother and father due to financial problems my mother live in Bihar and work there as agriculture worker .Today I am going to tell you all what I have experienced recently. Few months before, it was my class 10th Board exam. I used to go with my friends for exams. As usual, one day I was going to take my exam , I had faced an unsual weather condition. It was a hot weather accompanied by a hot sunny day. Everywhere ,there was a larks of heat . It seemed like the roads were burning , trees were withering and birds and animals as well as people were facing stiff condition. It was due to the climate change which was increasing from last few years. The factors that affect the climate change are , global warming, afforestation, emission of harmful smoke which includes Sulphur dioxide and Carbon monoxide from vehicle and industries. Due to this, it results in melting of glaciers, soil errosion, rising of earth's temperature, green house effect and depletion of Ozone layer (which protects earth's organisms from sun's ultraviolet rays). And we humans are the main cause of climate change. We are very ashamed of this condition. We should follow appropriate steps to control climate change.
