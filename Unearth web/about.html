About Us

This project was born out of
a desire to hear the stories and elevate the voices of youth in the Global South
who are deeply impacted by climate change.




The catastrophic floods in Pakistan, the melting glaciers in the Arctic, and the loss of biodiversity in the Amazon.... all share the same message from our precious planet. Humans are directly contributing to the Earth's rising temperature, and we have a finite amount of time to course-correct before its too late.


What will it take?


Everything. And all of us. We have to re-imagine how we live, work, travel, eat, build, produce, consume, transport, and communicate. Every story matters. Every voice needs to be heard, especially the most vulnerable young voices from the Global South. The Earth is calling. It's time to listen to those most impacted...and act.
