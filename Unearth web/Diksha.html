Diksha Kholiya

10th grade, Ambedkar Nagar,India


The Earth wants to cry

The earth seems sad, it is very quiet - said the Moon You are right, he has a bad face, the green of his face is disappearing - said the Sun Just like the blue of her cute face, it looks dirty, her beauty is disappearing! - exclaimed Venus The people who live there don't seem to take good care of it, he said: Mars And they all approached the earth to ask what was wrong with him? Hello Earth! Are you OK? For days we see you sad, wanting to cry, Are you okay? - said the moon The Earth turned her face with teary eyes and could not answer What concern, you are causing us Earth, we want to see you happy again and smiling, what's wrong? asked mars Tell us! they all insisted I'm worried, my green is gone and my blue isn't as shiny as it used to be. People hurt me, they don't seem to love me anymore - answered the Earth very sad The Moon, the Sun, Mars and Venus looked at her in silence with tears in their eyes, sad about what happened to him. Men are burning forests, cutting down trees, filling the sea with garbage, exterminating the animals - said the Earth sobbing Global warming is thawing my poles. How come they don't notice? They are destroying nature! they hurt themselves too in a few years no they will be able to live more here - he said starting to cry Jupiter came up and said: throw them out tell them to go away I will not let them live here besides I don't think I can, they should value the land and take care of her - said Mercury Some children who were playing in the woods overheard the conversation and with all their force they yelled at him Don't cry anymore, please! The earth listened to them - call me little children - it told them rinsing their tears We will take care of you and we are not going to allow them to cut down your trees, to dirty your seas, that they extinguish your animals. We will clean the streets and the seas let's go to recycle! the children promised in a mighty voice. The earth felt a great peace when it heard the promise of the children and I hoped they wouldn't forget their promise The children let others know about the damage they were doing to the Earth and Seeing him clean and recycle like this over time all the people began to care the earth The very happy and shining earth thanked them The Sun, the Moon, Mars, Venus, Mercury, and Jupiter, celebrated with her and they resumed their faith in humanity. They congratulated you! Thank you for helping me and caring about me - said the Earth, now I am very happy and healthy than ever.
