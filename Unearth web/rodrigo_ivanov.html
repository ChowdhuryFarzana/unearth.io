A TALK WITH MY GRANDFATHER

November 15, 2050 Petén, Guatemala. -Grandma, why is it so hot now? -Look son, a long time ago, the heat was not like now, before many of the days were with a temperate climate. -But then if the days were temperate, why is it so hot now? -I will tell you the story of us human beings and what we did to make the world so hot and there are so many natural disasters. A few hundred years ago, we began to pollute the world, throwing away our garbage in the wrong places, causing it to go into the rivers and then end up in the sea and thus, contaminate nature. By throwing the garbage in the wrong place, without realizing it we were making a hole in the ozone layer, this was the one that covered us from the direct rays of the sun, little by little we were destroying and at some point in life, it was so thin that the sun's rays began to melt the poles, which were a large part of the planet that was covered in ice and snow, little by little Little by little it melted and the little animals that lived there were dying because of the us. When we begin to realize our mistake, we all reflect, but after a few months, people "forgot" what was going on, it got to the point where that the poles practically no longer existed, after that, the sun's rays made the forests began to burn one by one and that made us now in a moment critical, in which there is almost no pure oxygen. -And what is oxygen abu? - It is the air we breathe, it is the most important element for living beings, without it we cannot we could live Thank you for clarifying that for me. -The truth, my son, is that if I could have changed everyone's thinking, I would have done it, without However, I did my part, planted trees and took care of the planet. -Grandma, if you could have changed the future of the world, what would you have done? Well... -I would have made human beings recycle more and remind my loved ones every day of the importance of caring for our planet, so that all young people and children today, They will enjoy nature and that rich climate that existed in those days. -Wow grandma, it would have been wonderful to live in that time, could you tell me more? -Yes my love, but the other stories will be for another day, sleep now, because tomorrow you have to go to school. -O yes and I will tell all my friends the story of how the planet was one day, thank you very much for all grandpa, I will also start taking care of my planet, I want to see it clean and enjoy a delicious weather. -My child is sleeping, because tomorrow will be another day. -See you tomorrow, Grandma.
