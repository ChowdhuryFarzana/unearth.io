Life is green

Once upon a time there was a blue planet, inhabited by seven and a half billion people, some of those people thought that the planet was theirs, they felt owners of plants, animals, trees, air, oceans, believed that the earth's resources were inexhaustible, they considered themselves the owners of all the earth and that they had the right to rule over other creatures. On the other hand, there was another group of people who became aware of the land, They were smart and the only thing they saw was not to lead their home to destruction, these people were making a global change movement every day, but they could never make the group of people who believed they owned the land could realize the damage they were doing to him, these people, despite the fact that they They believed they owned everything, they never did to take care of the land and see it with a value special, they threw garbage, they even burned it, they said that planting trees was a waste of time, they trimmed the trees, cut the plants and made many destructive things for the planet earth, which they did not realize was that little by little they were damaging it, it was getting hotter in some countries and the sowings no longer gave their fruits. But the good people and who loved their planet earth, they did not give up, they continued with their movement and for luckily more people joined them, they proposed to have a planet clean and pick up all the trash they could. One day, people with a good conscience towards the earth met the bad people, it was a very hot day, the good people talked to they made them see the damage they were doing to planet earth and that As a result of their bad treatment of the land, they were getting hot, not only the planet was affected, if not also its inhabitants, the bad people they had a moment of reflection and realized that they were right, that believing themselves to be the owners of everything and treating planet earth badly only made them feel was ending, they convinced themselves to be good to their home and They promised to take care of it, they began to make the change, picking up the garbage, planting more trees, etc. And they joined the movement, to convince more people about the harm that they do to the earth, luckily, more and more people raised their heads voice to claim their right to inherit a clean, fertile, fair and sustainable. ”We are still in time to change the destiny of the Planet, it is only necessary that you also join this movement for global change.”
